Privacy Policy for SYNC

Last Updated: March 15, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use SYNC, we collect the following information:

  • Google Account Information: Your name, email address, and profile picture when you sign in with Google.
  • Subscription Status: We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect your device ID and purchase history.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and maintain your account.
  • To determine your subscription status and grant access to premium content.
  • To remember your reading progress locally on your device.

3. Data Storage

  • Your account information is stored securely in Google Firebase.
  • News articles are stored for a maximum of 24 hours and then permanently deleted.
  • Your reading history is stored only on your device (never uploaded to our servers).

4. Third-Party Services

SYNC uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication and database
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management
  • Google Sign-In — Authentication

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties except as required by law.

6. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email directly. Your data will be deleted within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

SYNC is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
