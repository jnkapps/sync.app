SYNC Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 15, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use SYNC, we collect the following information:

  • Google Account Information: Your name, email address, and profile picture when you sign in with Google.
  • Subscription Status: We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect your device ID and purchase history.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate you and maintain your secure account.
  • To determine your subscription status and grant access to premium content.
  • To remember your reading progress locally on your device.

3. Data Storage

  • Your account information is stored securely in Google Firebase.
  • News articles are cached for a maximum of 24 hours and then permanently deleted.
  • Your reading history and "All Caught Up" status are stored only on your local device and are never uploaded to our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

SYNC uses the following third-party services to operate effectively:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication and database (Privacy Policy)
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Sign-In — Authentication (Privacy Policy)

5. Data Sharing

We value your privacy. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties, except as required by law.

6. Data Deletion

You have the right to your data. You can request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email provided below. Your data will be fully deleted within 30 days of your request.

7. Children's Privacy

SYNC is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
